ABOUT






Surigao Del Norte is a province in the Philippines situated in the Caraga Region Occupying the northeastern section of Mindanao. Its Capital is the city of Surigao. Surigao Del Norte is bordered clockwise from the North, by the Surigao Strait, Dinagat Islands, Dinagat Sound, Philippine Sea, Surigao Del Sur, Agusan Del Norte, Lake Mainit, and the Bohol Sea. Surigao Del Norte has 20 municipalities and 1 city. The total number of barangays in the province is 335.

This website wants to introduce to you the province of Surigao Del Norte, and will show some of beautiful places within the province.

Lets discover places in Surigao Del Norte and be amazed with its beauty!
