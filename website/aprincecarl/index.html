MY EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND

Primary

I received my primary education at San Pedro Elementary School in the Philippines and was fortunate enough to graduate in 2016. It was a wonderful experience to be a student at SPES and I am grateful for the knowledge and skills that I gained during my time there.

Secondary

I attended secondary school at Alegria National High School in Alegria, Surigao del Norte for grades 7 and 8 before transferring to Lilo-an National Technical Vocational High School in Lilo-an, Southern Leyte. I graduated from LNTVHS in 2021 and returned to Alegria to attend Senior High School at Alegria Stand Alone Senior High School, where I graduated in 2022.

Tertiary

I am currently pursuing my tertiary education at Surigao Norte State University, where I am a BSCS student. It is a privilege to have the opportunity to continue my studies at such a reputable institution, and I am excited for all that I will learn and achieve as I work towards my degree.
